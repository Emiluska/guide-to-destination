History

Seoul is the famous capital of South Korea the home of the famous Kpop groups such as BTS and Ateez and lets not forget about the amazing dramas. Seoul in the past has been the capitals of many kingdoms such as in the Joseon Period

Seoul Map

Places to Visit

There are a lot of places to visit such as the Bukchon Hanok Village to experience what it was like to live in South Korea during the Joseon Dynasty. For your favourite korean cosmetics you should shop at Myeongdong. If you want a thrill activity yout should ride the world's steepest wooden roller coaster at Everland.

Cuisine

In Korea you could taste the famous Kimchi (fermented vegatables), Bibimbap (mixed rice), Bossam(steamed pork). If you are feeling adventurous you could try the Sannakji (live octopus), Gejang (raw crabs)

Weather

The weather in Korea depending on when you go can be harsh. with in the summer months being very hot to winter months where it is very cold and dry, you should also be aware that a lot of the polluted air from china can travel there.
