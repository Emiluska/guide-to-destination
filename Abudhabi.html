Abu Dhabi

History

Abu Dhabi is the capital of United Arab Emirates where it has rich oil fields with neighbouring Dubai. Visit the set of the Fast and Furious franchise.

Abu Dhabi Map

Places to Visit

Visit the yas island where you can experience racing circuit and theme parks for an adrenalin filled trip while also visiting the Ferrari World Abu Dhabi where it holds the best motorsport-themed entertainment. For the best relaxation it is best to stay in the Emirates Palace Hotel where you can have a private marina.

Cuisine

While staying in Abu Dhabi you will have the opportunity to try camel meat with other different foods such as Majboos, Malqluba and Asida

Weather

Throughout the year the temperature rarely ever goes below 20C and in the summer months such as in August temperatures can reach to 35C
